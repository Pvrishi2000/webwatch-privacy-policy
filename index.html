Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2026

WebWatch is a Chrome extension developed by Rishi. Your privacy is important to us.

Information We Collect

WebWatch stores:

  • Website domains you visit.
  • Time spent on each website.

This information is stored locally on your device.

How We Use This Information

  • Display website usage statistics.
  • Help users understand browsing habits.
  • Improve productivity.

Data Storage

All browsing information remains on your computer using Chrome Storage. WebWatch does not upload your browsing history to any server.

Data Sharing

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not share your data.
  • We do not transfer your data to third parties.

Permissions

  • tabs
  • storage

These permissions are required only to measure website usage and save statistics locally.
